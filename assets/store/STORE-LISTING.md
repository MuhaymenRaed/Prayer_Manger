## Full description — English  (max 4000)
```
Yaqeen (يقين) is a Shia prayer companion built with love in Iraq. It brings you accurate Jafari prayer times, a qadha tracker to help you make up missed prayers, and gentle daily reminders — in a clean, bilingual (Arabic & English) app with no ads and no tracking.

✦ Accurate Shia prayer times
Jafari (Tehran-based) calculation tuned to match the trusted Iraqi timetables, with Fajr shown as الصبح, plus Sunrise, Sunset and the shar'i Midnight. Pick your city from Iraq's governorates and major Shia cities, or use GPS. Hide Asr & Isha if you pray them together.

✦ Qadha (missed-prayer) tracker
Set how many prayers you owe, make them up one by one, and watch your progress grow. Includes a shar'i calculator that estimates your missed years, plus a dedicated Salat al-Ayat counter.

✦ Takbir prayer alerts
Be notified at every prayer time with a real takbir sound — choose from 20 recitations or the standard notification tone. A pinned bar keeps the next prayer and its live countdown in your notification shade, even with the app closed.

✦ Daily Quran verse
A verse of the Quran delivered each day for reflection.

✦ Yours, and private
Sign in with Google or email to sync your tracker and settings across devices — your data is protected and never shared or sold. Prefer to stay offline? Everything works on-device too.

Made by Qcode. Follow @theyaqeen.iq on Instagram.
```
